First Aid at Work

This comprehensive course provides participants with the knowledge and skills to handle a wide range of workplace medical emergencies. It covers primary and secondary assessment, CPR, wound management, fractures, burns, shock, and unconscious casualties. Suitable for individuals designated as workplace first aiders, this training ensures compliance with health and safety regulations.

A

Emergency First Aid at Work

Designed for individuals requiring basic first aid training, this course covers essential life-saving skills such as CPR, choking response, bleeding control, shock management, and minor injuries. It is ideal for low-risk workplaces and equips participants with the confidence to act in emergency situations.

A

Basic Life Support & CPR

This course focuses on core life-saving skills, including cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR), the use of an automated external defibrillator (AED), and dealing with unconscious casualties. Participants will learn how to respond to cardiac arrest, choking, and breathing emergencies. Suitable for both workplace settings and the general public.

A

Fire Safety Awareness

This training provides essential knowledge on fire prevention, evacuation procedures, and the safe use of fire extinguishers. Participants will learn how to identify fire hazards, respond to fire emergencies, and implement safety measures in the workplace to reduce fire risks.

A

Manual Handling & Workplace Safety

A practical course focused on safe lifting techniques, risk assessments, and injury prevention in manual handling tasks. Participants will learn best practices to prevent musculoskeletal injuries and ensure compliance with workplace health and safety regulations. Ideal for employees in physically demanding roles.

A

Lone Working & Personal Safety

This course equips individuals working alone with the skills and awareness to stay safe in various environments. It covers risk assessment, emergency response planning, communication strategies, and handling potential threats or confrontations while working independently.
